My Story & Mission

Advocating for digital equality and delivering actionable solutions that bridge compliance gaps.

Empowering Inclusion

I started my digital accessibility journey after witnessing how standard user interfaces systematically lock out users with varying abilities. As a front-end developer, I realized that accessibility is not a compliance checklist; it's a fundamental human right.

Over the last decade, I have focused on translating dry, complex WCAG rules into practical, component-focused code revisions that engineers can quickly implement. I work on a personal basis with your engineering teams to provide both audits and the actual CSS/HTML/JS fixes needed.

IAAP Credentials

Certified Web Accessibility Specialist (WAS) and Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies (CPACC) by the International Association of Accessibility Professionals.

Compliance Frameworks I Support

WCAG 2.1 & 2.2 AA

The core international guidelines for layout contrast, non-text content, keyboard accessibility, and user input validation.

ADA Title III

Preventing litigation in the United States by securing public accommodations for commercial web services.

Section 508

Auditing and writing VPAT documentation for government agencies and organizations receiving federal funding.

I help companies audit, remediation-manage, and design web systems to follow WCAG 2.2 AA guidelines and ensure complete digital access.
