Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the auditing process, timelines, and developer remediation support.

A standard audit for a platform with 5 to 10 key templates typically takes 7 to 10 business days. This includes full manual testing across multiple screen readers, keyboard-only validation, and delivering your developer-ready remediation roadmap.

I do both. I provide a detailed PDF report highlighting each failure, but I also offer direct remediation support. Under this package, I deliver copy-pasteable HTML structure, CSS overrides, and JavaScript focus management scripts directly to your engineering team.

My services cover the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 and 2.2 at levels A and AA. Meeting these criteria ensures alignment with US regulations like ADA Title III and Section 508, as well as European standards under the European Accessibility Act (EAA).
